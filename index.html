關於我

從離開校園以來從事的工作皆與設計相關，設計是一門很有趣的學科，其牽涉的範圍也極為廣泛，只要我們在生活中多一點留心，大大小小的發現都能成為設計的亮點。

在從事設計工作中，主要工作經驗如軟體圖像icon設計、網站設計與維護、展場設計規劃、行銷設計文宣的製作等，工作上常常需要與PM、工程師溝通討論出最佳方案並且又能符合使用者易用性。當在從事設計的工作的時候，即便是繁瑣的細節也必須要嚴謹的去完成，才能讓最後的成果有完美的呈現，並且時常追求新知識。

設計的工作在於改善與創新，將概念具體化，將訊息圖像化，目的就是要利於使用者並為公司帶來營收，這也是我努力的目標。

About me

I have been working in the design field for many years. I thought it is an interesting subject and it related to the wide range. We pay attention to things in life that we always can find something to improve.

As I work in a design job, my responsibilities include icon/graphic design, web design and maintenance, marketing material design, and so on. Otherwise, I need to communicate with the sales manager, product manager, and engineer to produce the perfect solution also bring user-friendly. As a visual designer I always pay attention to details that make them elegant and pursue new knowledge.

The main idea of design is improvement and innovation. It is the translation from concept to substance, from information to graphic. The purpose is to bring benefit to the user and bring income to the company. That is what I am pursuing.
